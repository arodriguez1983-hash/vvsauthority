Lab Diamonds vs Natural Diamonds

Lab diamonds are diamonds created in a laboratory environment. They are commonly sold with grading reports when certified.

Understanding the 4 Cs

Carat, color, clarity, and cut are major grading factors shoppers use to compare diamonds.

IGI Certification

IGI reports document important grading details for many lab diamonds, including carat, color, clarity, and measurements.

Moissanite vs Diamond

Moissanite is a different gemstone from diamond and is known for strong fire and brilliance.

Gemstone Care

Store jewelry separately and clean pieces carefully based on stone type and setting.

Requesting a Stone

Provide stone type, shape, size, budget, and certificate preference so we can help match your needs.
