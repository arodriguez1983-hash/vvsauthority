Education

Learn the basics before buying diamonds, moissanite, gemstones, and jewelry.

Lab Diamonds vs Natural Diamonds

Lab diamonds have the same crystal structure as natural diamonds, but they are grown in a controlled environment.

Moissanite vs Diamond

Moissanite is a different gemstone known for strong fire and brilliance. It is popular for affordable sparkle.

IGI Certification

IGI reports can verify important stone details such as carat weight, color, clarity, measurements, and report number.

Gemstone Care

Different gemstones have different durability levels. Store pieces separately and clean gently to protect the finish.
